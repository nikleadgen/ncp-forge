# Your 52-Week Plan — From Detrained to the Podium

The whole campaign at a glance. Dates assume a **mid-June 2026 start**, peaking for the **online
qualifier (~mid-May 2027)** and the **New Christendom Press Games finals (~mid-June 2027, Ogden UT)**.
The app always shows you exactly where you are — this is the map.

**The shape of the year:** build the slow-to-fade qualities first (muscle, max strength, aerobic base),
sharpen the fast-to-fade ones last (power, speed, lactate), peak the *qualifier* lifts, then re-build
the *finals* events in a dedicated 5-week bridge. You train **4 core days/week**, with optional easy
days the app offers but never nags about.

**The one rule that protects it all:** never run hard the day before — or the hours before — a heavy
deadlift/squat day. Each block suggests a weekly layout that already does this for you.

---

### Block 1 — Foundation & Re-Entry · Weeks 1–8 (~Jun–Aug 2026)
**Goal:** re-teach the lifts light, build tendons & bones (they lag muscle by months), and lay an
aerobic base with run/walk. Win condition: arrive at week 9 *healthy with a base* — not sore.
- **Mon** Lower Strength · **Tue** Upper Strength · **Thu** Aerobic base (run/walk 60s/90s) · **Sat** Power intro + carries
- Lifts at RIR 3–5 (very submaximal). Grease-the-groove pull-ups/push-ups on optional days.
- **Deloads:** weeks 4 & 8 (every 4 weeks — recovery capacity is low early).
- **Test week 8:** deadlift triple, 1-mile TT, max pull-ups/push-ups, baseline "Cindy".

### Block 2 — Hypertrophy & Work Capacity · Weeks 9–16 (~Aug–Oct 2026)
**Goal:** build the muscle and rep-capacity everything stands on — more pull-ups & push-ups, a thicker
engine. Sandbag work is **carries + grip only** (explosive shouldering waits for a strength base).
- **Mon** Lower Hypertrophy · **Tue** Upper Hypertrophy + pulling · **Thu** Easy run + strides · **Sat** Carries, grip + metcon
- Lifts at RIR 1–3, higher volume. Aerobic maintained; strides add running economy cheaply.
- **Deloads:** weeks 12 & 16. **Test week 16.**

### Block 3 — Aerobic Engine & Durability · Weeks 17–22 (~Oct–Nov 2026)
**Goal:** a dedicated polarized (~80/20) block to build the engine the mile and every metcon run on.
Strength drops to **2 maintenance days** so it holds without blunting the aerobic adaptation. Sandbag
**shouldering technique** is introduced now that a base exists — light and crisp.
- **Mon** Lower (maintain) + sandbag technique · **Tue** Easy volume run · **Thu** Upper (maintain) · **Sat** Long easy + tempo finish
- "Easy" means *easy* — that's the point. Down week at week 22. **Test week 22.**

### Block 4 — Maximal Strength · Weeks 23–32 (~Nov 2026–Jan 2027)
**Goal:** turn muscle into force — the deadlift 1RM and heavy sandbag live here. Daily-undulating
(heavy / rep days). Endurance & muscular-endurance held at maintenance; one weekly power touch.
- **Mon** Deadlift Strength (+ box jumps) · **Tue** Upper Strength + pull-up strength · **Thu** Tempo run (maintain) · **Sat** Heavy sandbag + carries
- Top-set + back-off scheme on the main lifts; loads climb as your logged maxes climb.
- **5-week meso waves** with built-in deloads. **Test week 32.**

### Block 5 — Power, VO2max & Metcon · Weeks 33–42 (~Jan–Apr 2027)
**Goal:** convert strength to explosive power and get the mile *fast*. Strength shifts to maintenance
via speed deadlifts; VO2max intervals and real field-metcon rehearsals take center stage.
- **Mon** Lower power (speed pulls + jumps) · **Tue** VO2max intervals (6×400m @ mile pace) · **Thu** Upper power + pull/push volume · **Sat** Sandbag power + sprints + field metcon
- Sprints stay fully-recovered (quality, not fatigue). **Test week 42.**

### Block 6 — Qualifier Peak & Taper · Weeks 43–47 (~Apr–May 2027)
**Goal:** peak the two qualifier lifts — a max **deadlift** and **"Cindy"** — and arrive fresh.
- **Mon** Deadlift peak (heavy doubles climbing to max) · **Wed** Cindy practice + pacing · **Fri** Pull/push sharpen + OHP · easy aerobic to flush
- Volume drops hard the final two weeks; intensity/sharpness stay high.
- **▶ Week 47 = QUALIFIER WEEK:** submit a true 1RM deadlift + your best Cindy, fresh and primed.

### Block 7 — Bridge → Finals Peak · Weeks 48–52 (~May–Jun 2027)
**Goal:** the ~5 weeks between qualifier and finals — handled as a mini-cycle, **not** a long taper
(that would de-train the finals events). Recover → re-overload the fast-decaying finals qualities →
realization taper. The deadlift coasts on 1–2 heavy primers/week.
- **Wk 48** recover & re-stimulate · **Wk 49–50** finals overload: sandbag ladder sims, Norwegian 4×4
  mile work, field-metcon rehearsals · **Wk 51** taper
- **Mon** Sandbag ladder sim + DL primer · **Tue** Mile tune-up (4×4 min) · **Thu** Field metcon sim + sprints · **Sat** easy + mobility
- **▶ Week 52 = FINALS WEEK:** compete fresh. Trust the work. Come and conquer.

## How to actually use this

1. **Open the app, do the 5-tap readiness check.** It scales today's loads to how recovered you are.
2. **Hit Start.** Every weight, rep, and pace is already filled in — and adjusts to the numbers you log.
3. **Log a set, rest, repeat.** Tell it your RPE on hard sets so it can tune the next one.
4. **Finish** with a one-tap session difficulty. That feeds the weekly load monitor.
5. **Check Progress** every test week — your maxes, your mile, and your five event-readiness bars
   climbing toward the podium.

You bring the work and the honesty (log it like it happened). The app brings the plan and the math.
